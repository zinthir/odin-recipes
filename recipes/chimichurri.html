Chimichurri

Description

This Argentenian sauce is fresh, vibrant and tangy, delicious on meats such as steak.

A very simple recipe that will elevate just about any savory dish!

Ingredients

Yields 4-6 servings

  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons course sea salt
  • 4 garlic gloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 thai red chilis
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped flat-leaf parsely (about 1 bunch)/li>

Steps

  1. In a mortar and pestle, add sea salt, oil, and garlic. Grind until garlic becomes a smooth paste. A food processor also works.
  2. Add other ingredients and stir to combine. Ideally allow to sit for at least 2 hours, and may be made ahead of time and refrigerated for 24 hours.
  3. Serve with meats such as chicken or beef. Particularly great with grilled steaks.
